FUEL PASS USAGE INSTRUCTIONS

  • The National Fuel Pass portal was formally unveiled by the Power and Energy Ministry for easy and efficient fuel distribution.

  • The first time users should visit the fuel quota management portal available on the http://fuelpass.gov.lk.
  • Register on the website by entering your mobile number after your National Identity Card (NIC), passport, or business registration number.

  • Once the registration is complete, an OTP (One Time Password) will be sent to the registered mobile number for verification purposes.

  • Then the vehicle details such as vehicle number, type, chassis number and the vehicle type should be on the next page.

  • A QR code will show up on the screen after the page has been finished.After the completion of registration, you will be entitled for a fuel quota, balance remaining and date of expiry.

  • The QR code that appears on the screen can be downloaded and is specifically assigned to your vehicle.

  • You can present the QR code to the filling station when availing the service. If you are a smartphone user, take a screen shot and present it to the filling station attendant.

  • If not, you can present a print version instead. It is important to check the avaliable fuel quota balance by typing 'FUEL BAL (VEHICLE NO)'and sending it to 0766220000.

  • If you have already registered,you can log-in, clicking on Log-in
